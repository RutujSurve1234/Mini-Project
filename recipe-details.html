Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 4 tbsp honey
  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 scoop whey protien
  • 2 eggs

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large bowl, cream together the butter and honey until smooth.
  3. Stir in the eggs, milk and vanilla.
  4. Mix the wet ingredients and dry ingredients and make a dough.
  5. Shape the cookie dough and bake them.
